Quick Guide

How To Use The Schedule

Adding An Employee

  1. Click Add Employee.
  2. Enter the employee info, fixed day off, and target hours.
  3. Choose the Saturday group that matches their default rotation.
  4. Save the employee and the schedule row updates right away.

Helper Rows

  • Click Add Helper Row when outside help is coming in.
  • Helper rows start as neutral blank helper cells and do not affect hours or coverage until you schedule them.
  • Only change the days that are actually being used, and use Dark Cell on the unused days if you want them blacked out.
  • Put the helper’s name in the note and assign their shift for that day.

Saturday Groups

Group 1 is the default A/C-side rotation. New employees in Group 1 work Saturday on Week A and Week C, and are off Saturday on Week B.

Group 2 is the default B-side rotation. New employees in Group 2 work Saturday on Week B and are off Saturday on Week A and Week C.

Custom week type does not auto-assign Saturday. It leaves Saturday open for manual planning.

Editing Cells

  • Click a cell to open the editor.
  • Use the editor to change shift, PTO, notes, colors, and tasks.
  • Dark Cell blacks out a cell without counting hours or staffing.
  • 7:30 shifts are available for special manual schedules only. If anyone is manually set to 7:30, that day’s opening time becomes 7:30.
  • Ctrl/Cmd + click multiple cells to bulk edit them together.
  • Double click sets a cell to OFF. Double click an OFF cell again to restore it. Triple click sets it to PTO.

Auto Fill Coverage

  • Auto Fill rebuilds shifts to target hours first, then assigns opener, closer, night drop, and drive thru.
  • Auto Fill uses normal opening schedules. It does not create 7:30 shifts on its own.
  • Opener must start at opening time and have at least one other person with them.
  • Closer must stay until closing time and have at least three coworkers closing too.
  • Mike and Storm are excluded from Night Drop and Drive Thru.

Saving Work

  • Save File downloads the current schedule as a JSON file you can keep anywhere.
  • Open File restores a previously saved JSON file.
  • Cmd/Ctrl + S saves the current schedule file quickly.

Shortcuts

  • Cmd/Ctrl + S saves the current schedule file.
  • Cmd/Ctrl + Z undoes the last change.
  • Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + Z or Ctrl + Y redoes the change.

More Menu

  • Tutorial reopens this guide.
  • Reset Tasks clears task tags but keeps the shifts.
  • Reset Schedule rebuilds the week from the current employee setup.

Good Rule Of Thumb For New Employees

If the new employee should follow the same side as the current Week A / Week C Saturday crew, pick Group 1. If they should follow the Week B side, pick Group 2.
